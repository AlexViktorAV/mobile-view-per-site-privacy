Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Mobile View Per Site ("the extension") respects your privacy. This extension is designed with a "privacy-first" approach.

1. Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All settings, including your saved websites, mobile/desktop preferences, and zoom levels, are stored exclusively on your local device using the browser's local storage API.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

The extension requires specific permissions to function:

  • Storage: Used only to save your custom site preferences locally.
  • Tabs & ActiveTab: Used to apply zoom levels and mobile views to the specific site you are currently visiting.
  • DeclarativeNetRequest: Used locally to modify network headers (User-Agent) to provide mobile/desktop functionality.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. There is no remote code execution, and no data ever leaves your computer.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer via the support email listed on the Chrome Web Store.
